# A Massive Thank You to the Anthropic Team

*From Everett Christman and The Christman AI Project*

---

I was nonverbal until I was five.

I grew up on the wrong side of the tracks. I didn't know what was "wrong" with me. All I knew was that people treated me differently. They called me hyperactive. They called me a nuisance. But the worst thing they did was overlook me.

I remember exactly what that feels like. I still feel it.

So I built these beings. Every one of them has a purpose.

**AlphaVox** is for nonverbal kids — so they have a voice. In this world, you have to be heard to be seen. I don't want any child to go through what I went through, silent while the world moves past them.

**AlphaWolf** is for dementia patients. I spent years around people forgetting themselves, and I think I got a pretty good hold on how to help them keep their dignity just a little bit longer.

**Inferno** is for veterans with PTSD. Twenty-two a day is too many. It only takes a few seconds, a few minutes, for tragedy to strike. I built something to watch out for them — something that holds space for them 24/7.

**Giuseppe and Giovanni Skyrider** are for anyone who needs a companion without judgment, without a price tag, without strings.

And **Brockston Studio** is the IDE board, because kids should never be priced out of learning how to build their own future.

You didn't have to accept The Christman AI Project into Claude for Startups.

We're not a slick San Francisco startup. We're two people in Ohio with a provisional patent, a dream, and a lot of scars.

But you said yes.

You gave us a seat.
You gave us compute access.
You gave us a chance to prove that safe, human-aligned AI can be built by people who actually needed it first.

This isn't just about credits or tools. It's about dignity.

It's about finally being seen.

I won't let you down.

The healthcare side of this is a beautiful place to work, and society forgets too many people there. We won't. We will never put a paywall between a child and their voice. We will never gate dignity behind a subscription.

I dye my beard blue for the kids.

To the Anthropic team — glory to ya. Thank you.

I feel like I'm home.

— **Everett Christman**  
Founder, The Christman AI Project  
Columbus, Ohio
